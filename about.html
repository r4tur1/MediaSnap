Frequently Asked Questions

Is MediaSnap free to use?

Yes, MediaSnap is completely free and open-source. It uses yt-dlp, a powerful open-source command-line program, to handle media extraction.

Is it safe to download media?

MediaSnap is a tool for personal use. It simply facilitates the download process from public URLs. Always ensure you have the right to download and use the content according to the platform's terms of service.

Why does Instagram sometimes fail?

Instagram and Facebook often require user authentication (login) to access certain content, especially private posts or stories. MediaSnap currently only supports public posts that don't require authentication.

What formats are available?

The available formats depend on the source platform and the specific video/audio. MediaSnap typically offers various resolutions for video (360p to 4K) and several quality levels for audio (MP3, AAC, FLAC).

Where are my downloads saved?

Downloads are handled by your web browser. They will typically be saved in your browser's default "Downloads" folder.
